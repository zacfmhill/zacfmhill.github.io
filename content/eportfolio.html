Reflective Essay

My portfolio highlights how I've grown into someone who combines strong technical skills with a passion for solving meaningful problems. Early research in swarm robotics allowed me to gain skills in teamwork and communication, while projects like building an autonomous vehicle showed me how to apply creativity under real-world constraints. Both taught me rapid, user oriented, design and effective team collaboration. Altogether, these experiences shaped me into an engineer who brings technical rigor, creativity, and purpose to all their work.
